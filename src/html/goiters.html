Goiters

FAT & FUN

Goiters is a funky sans-serif typeface perfect for when you need to draw lots of attention without being boring or tacky. Though it's not recommended, it can also be used in small blocks of text such as this one.

Buy on Future Fonts

Playful Hero

Goiters is the product of jumping straight into vector drawing without looking at any other typefaces while doing work. The very first version of Goiters was created for marketing material for a hackathon for fonts called Type Jam. Go read more about that event if you'd like.

One of the constraints of Goiters was to never look at other typefaces early on in the process and just draw some fun looking stuff.

Quirky

Since Goiters was initially created for headings in the marketing material for Type Jam, one of the main focuses while working on it was drawing attention to important information with boldness and quirkiness.
